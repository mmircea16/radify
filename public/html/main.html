Radify

Guide your progress using a radar

Radify allows you to visualize your progress in an area of your life using a radar. A radar is a way of organise yourself towards achieving a goal. It helps you monitor which techniques, actions are helpful and which are not.

Log in to get started

Segments

Allows you to categorize the areas of improvement. For example, if you are monitoring your progress in being healthy, you could split your progress in segments, like "nutrition", "exercise", "stress" or "habit".

Blips

Blips are ways to achieve the goal. They can be a type of food you want to try or keep eating, a new habit you want to adopt, a workout you want to try, etc.

Tiers

Reflects how much an action or technique is helping you to achieve the goal
